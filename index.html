Airflow Through an RC Aircraft

Calculating airflow velocity is crucial for assessing the performance of remote-controlled (RC) aircraft. The process begins with creating a detailed 3D model of the aircraft, capturing its shape and aerodynamic features. Ansys Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) module is then utilized to simulate the airflow around the aircraft. This simulation incorporates variables such as air density, pressure, and temperature, which influence velocity distribution. Boundary conditions are set to replicate real-world operating conditions and environmental factors impacting the aircraft. Ansys solves the Navier-Stokes equations iteratively, predicting airflow patterns and velocities across the aircraft's surface. Post-processing tools in Ansys facilitate the extraction of key velocity data points. These velocity data points play a crucial role in evaluating the aircraft's performance and guiding optimization efforts. Ultimately, Ansys provides a robust toolkit for accurately estimating airflow velocity, thereby enhancing the design and aerodynamic efficiency of RC aircraft.

Smart Dustbin

The Smart Dustbin project integrates ESP32 Wi-Fi Module and Ultrasonic Sensors to revolutionize waste management practices. By leveraging IoT technology, the system enables real-time monitoring and efficient management of dustbins. Ultrasonic sensors accurately measure the fill levels of the bins, transmitting data via the ESP32 module to a centralized system. This connectivity allows operators to remotely monitor fill levels, optimize collection routes, and schedule timely pickups. Automatic alerts notify maintenance teams when bins require emptying, ensuring proactive servicing. The project not only enhances operational efficiency but also promotes sustainability by reducing unnecessary waste collection trips and optimizing resource usage. Overall, the Smart Dustbin project exemplifies the fusion of IoT innovation with practical waste management solutions to create cleaner, smarter cities.

Animal Tracking System

The Animal Tracking System harnesses the power of Arduino microcontrollers, GPS technology, and the SIM800L GSM module to monitor and track animals in real-time. This innovative system offers a reliable solution for wildlife conservation and livestock management. Equipped with GPS, the system accurately pinpoints the location of animals, allowing researchers, conservationists, and farmers to monitor their movements and habitats remotely. The SIM800L GSM module enables seamless communication over GSM networks, transmitting location data to a centralized server or mobile application. This connectivity ensures real-time updates on animal whereabouts and enables proactive measures in case of emergencies or incidents. The system is designed for durability and efficiency in diverse environments, providing robust tracking capabilities even in remote or rugged terrain. By facilitating precise tracking and monitoring, the Animal Tracking System supports conservation efforts, enhances livestock management practices, and contributes to the sustainable use of natural resources.
